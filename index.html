Money and financial support

Find out how much severance pay you are owed

If you were sent home from your job, your employer may owe you severance pay. This tool works out how much.

What to have ready

Have these ready before you start:

  • Why you were sent home.
  • How many complete years you worked there.
  • Your usual weekly or monthly pay.

How long does it take?

About 3 minutes.

Start now

You can use this tool if

  • You worked for the same employer for at least 2 years.
  • You were between 16 and 64 years old on your last day at work.
  • You were sent home because your job was cut, your workplace was damaged by a disaster, or you had no work for a long time.

This tool may not apply if

  • You were dismissed because of your own conduct.
  • You turned down a suitable job offer from the same employer.
  • Your type of work is not covered under the Severance Payments Act.

Do not wait too long

You must make your claim within 12 months of your last day at work. If you wait longer, you may lose your right to severance pay.

About your estimate

The amount shown is the minimum your employer must pay under the Severance Payments Act. Your contract of employment may entitle you to more.

If your employer does not pay, contact the Ministry of Labour, Social Security and Third Sector.
